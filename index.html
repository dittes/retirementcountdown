Retirement Information

About This Website

The Retirement Countdown provides a personalized visualization of your journey to retirement. By entering your birth date and desired retirement age, you can track the exact time remaining until this important milestone.

Retirement Ages in the USA

In the United States, retirement ages vary based on several factors:

  • Social Security Full Retirement Age: Between 66 and 67, depending on birth year
  • Early Retirement: Available from age 62 (with reduced benefits)
  • Delayed Retirement: Up to age 70 (with increased benefits)

Social Security Benefits

Your Social Security retirement benefit is based on:

  • Your lifetime earnings history
  • The age at which you start receiving benefits
  • Your work duration (minimum 40 credits needed)

Retirement Planning Tips

Consider these key aspects when planning for retirement:

  • Start saving early through 401(k)s and IRAs
  • Diversify your retirement portfolio
  • Consider healthcare costs and Medicare eligibility
  • Plan for inflation and cost of living adjustments
